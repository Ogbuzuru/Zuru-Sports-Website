Privacy Policy

Zuru International Limited (RC: 1756003) · Last updated: 26 February 2026

← Back to home

Who we are: Zuru International Limited (RC: 1756003) operates the Zuru Sports app. Contact: support@zurusports.com. Governed by the Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023 (NDPA).

1. Data We Collect

  • Name and email — at registration
  • Phone number — for profiles
  • Profile photo — voluntary; Firebase Storage
  • Business name and address — from managers
  • Payment records — amounts, references, wallet history. Never card numbers or CVVs.
  • App usage data — bookings, cancellations, wallet activity
  • State and city — manually entered; no GPS collected

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To create and manage your account
  • To process bookings and payments via Paystack
  • To manage your Zuru Wallet
  • To send booking confirmations and support responses
  • To verify facility manager accounts
  • To detect and prevent fraud
  • To comply with Nigerian law including the NDPA 2023

We do not use your data for advertising or sell it to any third party.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Paystack — PCI-DSS Level 1, CBN-licensed.
  • Firebase / Google — Auth, database, storage. GDPR-compliant.

4. Data Sharing

  • With Paystack — email and amount only
  • With Firebase/Google as cloud infrastructure
  • With Nigerian regulatory or law enforcement bodies when required by law

5. Retention

Profile data deleted within 30 days of account deletion. Payment records retained for 6 years per FIRS regulations.

6. Security

  • All data encrypted via HTTPS/TLS
  • Firebase Security Rules restrict access to authenticated users only
  • Paystack handles card data under PCI-DSS Level 1 — Zuru never sees raw card data

7. Your Rights (NDPA 2023)

  • Access, rectification, erasure of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Complain to NDPC at ndpc.gov.ng

DPO: support@zurusports.com. Response within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

Users under 13 must have parental or guardian consent to register and use Zuru Sports. Contact support@zurusports.com if a minor has registered.

1. About

Zuru International Limited (RC: 1756003) operates a technology platform connecting players with verified facility managers across Nigeria. We are a booking and payment intermediary — not a facility operator.

2. Eligibility

You are responsible for your account security. Notify support@zurusports.com immediately of any unauthorised access.

3. Bookings & Payments

Bookings confirmed only on successful Paystack payment. Prices in Naira (₦). Confirmed bookings are binding.

4. Cancellations & Refunds

Player Cancellations — Non-refundable

Confirmed bookings are non-refundable. No-shows forfeit their payment.

Manager Cancellations — Full Zuru Wallet Credit

Manager cancels = full automatic credit to all players' Zuru Wallets within minutes.

Late Cancellation Penalty (within 1 hour)

₦500 per booked player deducted from manager wallet — plus full player refunds.

5. Zuru Wallet

Credits usable within Zuru Sports only. Non-transferable, non-withdrawable. Expire 12 months after issue.

6. Manager Earnings

Managers receive a percentage of session revenue as specified in the Manager Agreement or the current fee schedule published in the app. Payouts to a registered bank account within 1–3 business days.

7. Prohibited Activities

  • Unlawful use under Nigerian law
  • Listing fraudulent sessions
  • Processing payments outside Zuru
  • Creating multiple accounts to abuse systems
  • Scraping or reverse-engineering the app

To the fullest extent permitted by Nigerian law, Zuru is not liable for personal injury during sessions, indirect loss, or technical failures beyond our reasonable control.

9. Governing Law

Governed by Nigerian law. Disputes unresolved in 30 days go to arbitration under the Arbitration and Conciliation Act (Cap A18, LFN 2004).

All refunds on Zuru Sports are issued as Zuru Wallet credits — not cash.

Player Cancellations

No refund

Confirmed bookings are non-refundable. Players who cancel or do not attend forfeit their payment.

Manager Cancellations

Full Zuru Wallet credit — automatic

Full credit to all players' Zuru Wallets within minutes of cancellation.

Late penalty (within 1 hour)

₦500 per booked player deducted from manager wallet — on top of full player refunds.

Waitlist Players

Automatic refund if not confirmed

Unconfirmed waitlist payments automatically refunded to Zuru Wallet after session ends.

Wallet Credits

  • Usable for Zuru Sports sessions only
  • Non-transferable, non-withdrawable as cash
  • Expire 12 months after issue

Payment Processing

All payments processed by Paystack (CBN-licensed). Completed transactions cannot be reversed directly — all refunds are Zuru Wallet credits.
